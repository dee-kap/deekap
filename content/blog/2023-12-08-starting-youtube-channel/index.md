This is something I have been thinking about for some time but never got around to it. Work and other pursuits kept me from diving into it, not to mention procrastination.

For my [first video](https://youtube.com/shorts/2ZOi9xZx2PA?si=DUq0fZWBdS12khrZ), I wanted to start with the basics, so I created a video about how to get English alphabets in Python.
