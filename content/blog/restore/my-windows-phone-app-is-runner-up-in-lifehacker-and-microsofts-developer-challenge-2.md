Found an uplifting email today in my mailbox which informed me that my app for Windows Phone has been a runner-up in [round 5 of Lifehacker and Microsoft’s developer challenge](http://www.lifehacker.com.au/2013/05/round-5-winners-lifehacker-and-microsofts-developer-challenge/). I am very happy about this and I’d like to thank those who judged my app to be worthy of recognition.  I would also like to congratulate [Jared Homes](http://apps.microsoft.com/windows/en-ph/app/fair-football-coach/13eaa6b2-f12d-433a-8d50-5f5385c5ea99) who won round 5 and [Alistair Michener](https://twitter.com/almichener) who is also a runner-up for his awesome app [Drawboard PDF](http://apps.microsoft.com/windows/en-US/app/drawboard-pdf/6d65bcd8-8390-4533-af58-307d2e1ec1dd).

Thanks also to [Andrew Coates](http://blogs.msdn.com/b/acoat/) and [Dave Glover](http://blogs.msdn.com/b/dglover/) for their support and feedback.
