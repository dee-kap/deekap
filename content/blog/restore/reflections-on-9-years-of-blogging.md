I have been blogging for about 9 years. Most of that has been a below average effort. I will be honest and admit that it has been abysmal effort except a time period when I was actively blogging. This post is a reflection on all those years. Mostly on the mistakes I have made. If you were looking for an inspirational story then I'm sorry to disappoint you. Over the years I switched between many domain names. Somehow could not find the one I really liked. Most of them are not online. I went to the WayBack Machine to view the skeletons. My first blog was deepwinfx.com. I launched deepwinfx.com back when WinFX was a thing. It was part of the Vista disaster. I got myself into Longhorn (remember that) very early and planned to be an expert in it's pillars. I did spend few years working with WPF which I regret now. That time could have been better spent on learning other technologies. Wayback Machine has some information on deepwinfx.com but it seems like the domain was used by someone else for their business. Next came DeepakKapoor.net. This domain lived for the longest time online. I wrote some content I was proud of. From WayBack Machine the earliest screenshot I could get is from April 2005. ![Screen Shot 2017-08-28 at 1.51.51 pm](https://bitofbinary.files.wordpress.com/2017/08/screen-shot-2017-08-28-at-1-51-51-pm.png) Somewhere around 2009 I moved to onedotnetway.com. As Internet never forgets anything, I can find traces of onedotnetway on Google. After I moved on from onedotnetway.com, the domain was acquired by someone else. Then came a period of chaos when I moved by blogging to a different domain whenever the season changed. This was plain stupid. I also **had** a tendency of copying other people. I figured that cool kids were using .io domains so I went and got myself Kapoor.io in 2015 and moved my blog. Here is a screenshot of kapoor.io running Wordpress. ![Screen Shot 2017-08-31 at 2.16.34 pm.png](https://bitofbinary.files.wordpress.com/2017/08/screen-shot-2017-08-31-at-2-16-34-pm.png) And here it is on Jekyll. ![Screen Shot 2017-08-31 at 2.16.53 pm.png](https://bitofbinary.files.wordpress.com/2017/08/screen-shot-2017-08-31-at-2-16-53-pm.png) In terms of platforms I started with Wordpress then moved to Octopress then Jekyll and I'm back to Wordpress. A big mistake I now realise with all this domain changing was that I lost **gigalitres of Google Juice**. Had I remained with one domain throughout years, I'd be looking at a nice timeline of my blogging journey. It could have been a Window into my career, but I use a mac now. That was a joke in case you missed it. This is the history of my 12 years of blogging. Not the most inspiring but true. Now I feel that there is no point looking back and I'd like to start blogging afresh. Of course on a new domain. Some things just don't change. This time however I plan to stick with it and most likely BitOfBinary.com will be my permanent home for tech blogging.
