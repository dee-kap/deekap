CDNs are a popular way to improve a website's performance. The idea is that assets such as scripts, styles etc are loaded from domains other than your own domain. These endpoints are optimised to serve static assets and they do this well. It is common to use CDNs for popular JavaScript libraries and CSS frameworks such as bootstrap. This is a list of some popular CDNs which are available to you for free.

## Google Hosted Libraries

This is perhaps one of the most popular CDN out there. I use it for most of my projects. You can find AngularJS, DOJO, jQuery, jQuery UI, Prototype and few others. There is also a bit of comfort level associated with using Google's CDN. Being Google, chances of 404 are slim. https://developers.google.com/speed/libraries/  ![Screen Shot 2014 09 18 at 12 52 07 pm](http://kapoor.io/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Screen-Shot-2014-09-18-at-12.52.07-pm.png "Screen Shot 2014-09-18 at 12.52.07 pm.png")

## Microsoft Ajax Content Delivery Network

Another reliable CDN from one of the big tech companies. Their list of libraries is a bit different from Google's. At the time of this writing they do not serve AngularJS but you can find other popular libraries such as jQuery, Modernizr etc. http://www.asp.net/ajax/cdn  ![Screen Shot 2014 09 18 at 12 57 32 pm](http://kapoor.io/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Screen-Shot-2014-09-18-at-12.57.32-pm.png "Screen Shot 2014-09-18 at 12.57.32 pm.png")

## cdnjs

List of stuff you can find on cdnjs is massive. They host close to 900 libraries. There is a high chance that if a library you are using is even remotely popular then you will find it on cdnjs. http://cdnjs.com/  ![Screen Shot 2014 09 18 at 1 06 03 pm](http://kapoor.io/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Screen-Shot-2014-09-18-at-1.06.03-pm.png "Screen Shot 2014-09-18 at 1.06.03 pm.png")

## jsDelivr

Next on the list is jsDelivr. There is also a ton of stuff available on jsDelivr. They even have a node module you can use in your node.js applications. More details can be found on their [Github repository](https://github.com/jsdelivr/jsdelivr). http://www.jsdelivr.com/  ![Screen Shot 2014 09 18 at 1 03 32 pm](http://kapoor.io/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Screen-Shot-2014-09-18-at-1.03.32-pm.png "Screen Shot 2014-09-18 at 1.03.32 pm.png")

## Bootstrap CDN

The name says is all. This is one place to get your bootstrap css and JavaScript. They also host Font Awesome which is just awesome and Bootswatch which is a great collection of clean Bootstrap themes. http://www.bootstrapcdn.com/  ![Screen Shot 2014 09 18 at 1 00 19 pm](http://kapoor.io/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Screen-Shot-2014-09-18-at-1.00.19-pm.png "Screen Shot 2014-09-18 at 1.00.19 pm.png") I am sure that there are other good CDNs available. This list is based on the ones I have used and I continue to use. Drop me a line if you have a favourite and it is not on my list.
