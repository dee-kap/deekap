Arnold is a three part series on Netflix which I recently watched with my family.

I discovered Arnold Schwarzenegger through movies and I the first movie I saw of Arnold was Predator. After that, I did not miss any of his movies.

The series serves as an excellent introduction to the three parts of Arnold’s life. His body building career, becoming one of the biggest movie star and his political career in which he served as the governor of California.

Some of the things I learned about him.

1. He had a tough childhood because of his father but he looked up to his mother
2. He is extremely competitive
3. Has mad dedication for anything he gets into
4. He was a millionaire because of his real estate investments before he became a star
5. Because of his financial independence, he was able to make choices which delivered asymmetric growth in his life

I always found his life story to be interesting and inspiring and I plan to read his biography Total Recall next.
